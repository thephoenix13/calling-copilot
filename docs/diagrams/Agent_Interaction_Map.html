Step-by-Step Agent Execution Flow
1
Select JD — Intake Qualification
HM Q&A Generator
Job record → 8 structured intake questions for hiring manager call. Optionally regenerates individual questions. Output stored in session state.
2
Enhance JD — 6 Agents in Parallel ⚡ Promise.all
JD Parser Formatted JD Gen Recruiter Brief Gen Clarification Questions Gen Reachout Material Gen Sourcing Keywords Gen Market Intelligence Per-Question Regenerator
JD Parser runs first, then 6 generators fire concurrently via Promise.all. All outputs merged into enhancement_data session state. Per-Question Regenerator replaces a single clarification question on demand (sequential).
3
Source Candidates — Resume Processing
Resume Parser Resume Evaluator AI Content Detection
Resume Parser extracts structured fields from PDF/DOCX. Its output feeds two independent agents: Resume Evaluator (0–100 match score) and AI Content Detection (human/ai verdict). Both results stored per candidate.
4
Recruiter Screening — Live Call Loop
Interview Q Generator Twilio Voice Follow-Up Suggestions Candidate Simulation Dual Report Generator
Interview Q Generator produces question guide before the call. During the live call, Follow-Up Suggestions fire per recruiter turn (real-time, Haiku for latency). Candidate Simulation provides practice replies. After call ends, Dual Report Generator produces QA Report + Candidate Report in one Opus call.
5
Video Interview — Async Transcription Pipeline
Video Q Generator Deepgram Transcription Video Evaluator
Video Q Generator creates interview questions from JD. Candidate records video responses. Each response is transcribed via Deepgram (async, stored). Video Evaluator then evaluates all responses together — producing competency scores, hiring recommendation, per-question analysis.
8
Post-Offer Follow-Up — Scheduler Loop
Risk Scorer Email Trigger Logic POFU Email Generator SMTP Mailer
Risk Scorer calculates risk score deterministically (state + DOJ proximity + engagement recency). Email Trigger Logic selects the right email type from a 9-step sequence. POFU Email Generator drafts a warm, personalized email (Haiku for cost). Candidate reply → updates risk score → loop continues.

Model Tier Breakdown
7
Claude Haiku
Fast, cheap, low-latency.
Used for: real-time suggestions, resume parsing & scoring, AI content detection, POFU emails.
12
Claude Sonnet
Balanced quality + speed.
Used for: all JD enhancement agents, video interview Q&A, HM questions, JD refresh.
2
Claude Opus
Highest quality, complex output.
Used for: Interview Q Generator (multi-section guide), Dual Report Generator (two 8-dim reports).
