Two Breakdowns
Where agency revenue actually leaks

The funnel breaks on both ends —
and no one has fixed either.

Top of funnel

Recruiter calls don't scale.

  • Every agency has 1–2 strong recruiters and 8–12 average ones.
  • Delivery heads listen to 5–10 calls a week out of thousands. Coaching is anecdotal.
  • AI-generated resumes have broken pre-call signals. The call is now the only trustworthy signal.
  • New-recruiter ramp-up takes 60–90 days with no feedback loop.
Bottom of funnel

Accepted offers silently die.

  • Counter-offers from current employers — 30–40% of accepts.
  • BGV failures — discrepancies in dates, education, criminal checks.
  • Multiple-offer juggling — candidates pick one closer to joining.
  • Notice-period erosion in 60–90 day markets.
  • Post-acceptance ghosting — silent right up to Day-1.

Pillar 1 · Two Reports Per Call
Every call · Two artifacts

Recruiter and candidate, scored together.

Recruiter-facing

Per-Call QA Report

Scores the recruiter across five dimensions:

  • Coverage — was every required area probed?
  • Structure — was the call well-organized?
  • Tone & empathy — did the candidate feel respected?
  • Probing depth — surface answers vs. real signal?
  • Closing — next steps clearly set?

Color-coded score · evidence-grounded · weakest dimension flagged · targeted coaching nudges.

Candidate-facing

Candidate Evaluation Report

Structured assessment of fit based on what was actually said:

  • Skill match against required and preferred skills
  • Experience alignment — depth, relevance, recency
  • Motivation signals — interest vs. fishing
  • Red flags — inconsistencies, hesitation
  • Submission verdict — proceed / hold / reject

Powers the pipeline decision · submission notes write themselves.

Pillar 2 · Post-Offer Follow-Up
Post-Offer Follow-Up

The risk window
no ATS protects.

Most ATSes treat offer-accept as the finish line. It's actually the risk peak. POFU activates the moment a candidate accepts and stays active until Day-1.

A

Automated Check-ins

Personalized emails at the right intervals — T+3 days, T+1 week, mid-notice, T-7 before joining.

B

State Tracking

Captures progression: Resigned → BGV → Joining date → Joined. Missing milestones are signals.

C

AI Risk Scoring

Continuously updated low/medium/high risk. Inferred from response latency, sentiment, delays.

D

Intervention Nudges

When risk crosses a threshold, the system surfaces the candidate with a recommended action.

Background engine runs every 6 hours via node-cron · fully autonomous in steady state.
