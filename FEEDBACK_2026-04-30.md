## 1. Job Management

### 1.1 Bulk JD Upload
- Allow recruiters to upload multiple JDs at once (batch intake).

### 1.2 Requirement Qualification / Job Intake
- After a JD is added, recruiter goes to the HM with a structured set of clarification questions to understand the requirement in depth.
- Each clarification question must have a **Regenerate** button to re-generate an alternative question.
- Once all clarification questions are completed, the job is marked **Qualified**.
- On qualification, **all associated JD assets** (enhanced JD, Boolean string, outreach copy, etc.) must be automatically refreshed/updated.

## 3. Candidate Matching & Reports

### 3.1 Match Reasoning
- On the candidate report, show **"Why is this candidate a match?"**
- Display via hover tooltip or a modal — not inline text.

### 3.2 Status Terminology
- Replace Pass / Fail labels everywhere with:
  - **Shortlist**
  - **Reject**
  - **On Hold**

### 3.3 No Automated AI Decisions
- AI must **never make autonomous hiring decisions** anywhere in the platform.
- AI can surface recommendations, scores, and summaries — final decision always requires recruiter action.

---

## 4. Screening & Assessment

### 4.1 Assessment Selection at Screening Stage
- At the Screening stage, recruiter can choose which assessment to share with the candidate:
  - Interview
  - MCQ
  - Coding
  - SME Panel
- Selected assessment auto-generates an email with:
  - A direct link to the assessment.
  - Brief context about the role and what the recruiter discussed with the candidate earlier.
  - Keep context minimal for now — basic briefing only.

## 5. Video Interviews

### 5.1 Integration
- Integrate video interview tooling into RecruiterOS with platform-specific modifications.

### 5.2 Post-Interview Scoring
- Scoring is automated after the video interview is completed.
- Recruiter must review and take an action (Move Ahead / Hold / Reject) before the pipeline advances — no auto-progression.
- Display the full interview report alongside the scoring.

## 6. Pipeline Feedback

### 6.1 Voice Note Feedback (HM / SME)
- In-pipeline feedback for Hiring Managers and SMEs via **voice note**.
- Voice note is **auto-transcribed**.
- Transcription is:
  - Added to the candidate's profile under the specific JD.
  - Fed into the **Learning Loop** for future recommendation improvement.
